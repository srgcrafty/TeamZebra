Official Name and Species

The primary species is the plains zebra, the sub species would be the mountain zebra or the Grevy's zebra.

Interesting Facts

  • Distinctive striped coat
  • Several species African equids
  • Have never been truly domesticated
  • Zebras are very fast; they can reach 65 mph
